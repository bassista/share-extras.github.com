The software packages you will find on this site are designed to install on top of the Alfresco Share collaboration platform. All code is licensed under the Apache License 2.0.

We have a wide range of add-ons that seek to demonstrate the power of Share Extensions, plus a number of SDK projects to provide simple, best practice examples which you can use as a starting point for your own projects.

If you find the add-ons useful, please consider helping us by adding feedback on the Alfresco Add-ons site or by contributing to the projects themselves. We love pull requests!
